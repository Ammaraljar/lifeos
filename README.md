# LifeOS 🎯
نظام الحياة الشخصي — Personal Life Operating System

A Flutter app built around 6 life areas:
🕌 Spiritual | 🇬🇧 English | 💪 Fitness | 💼 Business | 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family | 📚 Knowledge

## Features
- Daily habit tracking with streaks
- Annual goals progress (Quran pages, English hours, prayer consistency)
- Arabic + English with instant RTL switching
- Dark & Light mode
- 100% offline
